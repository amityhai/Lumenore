Before you decide

Everything you need to know about plans, credits, and getting started with Lumenore.

Lumenore credits offer access to AI-powered analytics features like conversational analytics, predictive analytics and advanced analytics. Each query consumes credits based on data size and complexity. The Grow plan includes 300 credits, while the Essentials plan offers 3,000 credits per user.

Yes, the Grow plan is completely free, forever. It includes core AI features and community support to help you get started.

You can upgrade to the Essentials or Custom plan to access more features, credits, and resources.

Lumenore uses enterprise-grade encryption and complies with global security standards to ensure your data remains safe.
