The Non-Negotiables

  • 01 All headings use Space Grotesk 300. No heading may use Fraunces or Inter. No exceptions.
  • 02 Buttons are always 6px radius. Pills are always 50px. Do not deviate from these values.
  • 03 Section padding is 96px. Intro-to-content gap is 60px. These are not suggestions.
  • 04 Body text on white is #4a5568. On dark, rgba(252,252,252, 0.72). No other values.
  • 05 Use --Explore-Button for all gradient CTAs. Never redeclare the gradient inline in component CSS.
